# Macnman Technologies Pvt. Ltd. – Standard Warranty Terms & Conditions (2025 Updated)

## Introduction

Macnman Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (“Macnman”) is committed to delivering reliable, industrial-grade IoT devices designed for monitoring, automation, and long-term field operations. To support our customers and partners worldwide, Macnman provides a Standard Limited Warranty that ensures protection against manufacturing and workmanship defects.

This Warranty Policy applies globally. In case of conflict with local laws, local regulations will prevail.

This Limited Warranty applies exclusively to Macnman-branded products, including hardware and necessary embedded firmware. It does not cover:
- Non-Macnman software  
- Third-party sensors, modules, accessories, or peripherals  
- Products not manufactured or supplied by Macnman  

Macnman is not responsible for interoperability or compatibility issues arising from components, applications, or integrations not approved by Macnman.

## Warranty Coverage

### Standard Product Warranty (3 Years)
All Macnman hardware products are covered under a three (3) year warranty from the date of original purchase (invoice date), covering:
- Mechanical enclosures  
- Electronic components  
- RF circuitry and communication modules  
- Embedded firmware supplied by Macnman  
- PCB and internal hardware assemblies  

This warranty covers manufacturing defects only.

### Battery & Sensor Probe Warranty (Variant-Based)
Batteries and sensor probes are excluded from the 3-year warranty. Warranty duration depends on model and product category:
- Lithium battery cells: 6–12 months  
- Non-rechargeable batteries: No warranty  
- Soil moisture probes: 6–12 months  
- pH probes: 6 months  
- Gas sensor cartridges (H₂S, NH₃, CO₂, CH₄): 6–12 months  
- Optical, ultrasonic, and other detachable sensor probes: 12 months  

Final warranty duration for these items shall match each product’s datasheet.

### Warranty for Replaced or Repaired Parts
Repaired or replaced components are warranted for:
- 90 days, or  
- The remaining warranty period of the device  
Whichever is longer.

## 04. After-Sales Service & RMA Handling

### 4.1 RMA Request and Approval
Before returning any product, customers must request an RMA Number by emailing:  
**support@macnman.com**

Required details:
- Product model  
- Serial number / QR code / MAC ID  
- Purchase invoice  
- Fault description  
- Installation images (if applicable)  
- Logs or data (if applicable)

### 4.2 Shipping & Logistics
- **Customer → Macnman:** Customer bears shipping, packing, insurance & handling.  
- **Macnman → Customer:** Macnman covers shipping only for approved warranty repairs.  
- Out-of-warranty cases: Round-trip shipping paid by customer.

### 4.3 Inspection & Service
Macnman reserves the right to inspect all returned products. Warranty is void for:
- Physical damage  
- Improper installation  
- Broken/tampered warranty seal  
- Force majeure events

- Damage caused by insects, rodents, corrosion, or chemicals  

Service is subject to component availability.

### 4.4 Out-of-Warranty Repair Charges
Repair cost will be communicated in advance and approved by the customer before repairs begin.
