# Product Lifecycle & End-of-Life (EOL) Policy

Macnman Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is committed to providing long-term, reliable hardware solutions for industrial and mission-critical IoT deployments.  
This Product Lifecycle & EOL Policy defines how long Macnman manufactures, supports, and maintains each hardware product.

## Product Lifecycle Stages

Macnman follows four distinct lifecycle stages for every hardware product.

### Active Production
- Product is in full-scale manufacturing  
- Available for standard purchase  
- Receives firmware improvements and optimizations  
- Full warranty and RMA support available  

### Maintenance / Sustaining Phase
This phase begins as the product matures or when a successor model is introduced.

During this stage:
- Product availability continues with longer lead times  
- Firmware updates are limited to security & stability patches  
- No new features added  
- Component substitutions may occur due to supply-chain constraints  

### End-of-Life (EOL) Announcement
An official notice is issued when Macnman plans to discontinue a product.

The EOL notification includes:
- Last Order Date (LOD)  
- Last Shipment Date  
- End of Firmware Support date  
- Recommended replacement models  

### Post-EOL Support
After discontinuation:
- Warranty for already purchased units remains active  
- Paid repair service continues while spare parts are available  
- Firmware updates provided only for critical issues  

---

## Macnman Product Support Commitment

### Manufacturing Availability
Macnman will manufacture and supply each product for a **minimum of 5 years** after launch.  
Products with long-term demand may extend up to **7 years**.

### Firmware Support
- Bug fixes: **Up to 5 years** post-launch  
- Security patches: **Up to 7 years**, depending on MCU vendor support  
- No major new features added after Maintenance Phase  

### Spare Parts Availability
Spare parts remain available for **at least 3 years after the EOL announcement**.

---

## End-of-Life (EOL) Process

### Notification
Macnman publishes the EOL announcement to:
- Distributors  
- OEM partners  
- Enterprise customers  

### Last Buy Orders
Customers may place final orders until the **Last Order Date**.  
After this date:
- No new purchases  
- Only support and warranty remain active  

### Last Shipment
Macnman ships the last batch on the **Last Shipment Date**.

### Post-EOL Support
- Warranty honored as per original purchase  
- Paid repairs continue until parts are exhausted  
- Firmware updates limited to critical vulnerabilities  

---

## Reasons for EOL

A product may reach EOL due to:

- Component unavailability or semiconductor obsolescence  
- Introduction of next-generation models  
- Updated regulatory or certification requirements  
- Adoption of more advanced MCU/RF technologies  
- Reduction in market demand  
- Supply chain discontinuation  

---

## Customer Responsibilities During EOL

Customers should:

- Track EOL announcements  
- Plan procurement before the Last Order Date  
- Maintain buffer inventory for large deployments  
- Evaluate recommended replacement models  
- Update internal documentation and deployment plans  

---

## Replacement Product Guidance

Macnman provides:

- Drop-in or pin-compatible alternatives whenever possible  
- Migration guides for successor products  
- Updated datasheets and integration notes  
- Support for field migration strategies
