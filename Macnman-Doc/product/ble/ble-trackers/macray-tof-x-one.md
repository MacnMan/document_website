# Ble Tag X2

## Product Overview

**Ble Tag X2** is an **industrial-grade Bluetooth® Low Energy (BLE) asset tracker** designed for **real-time motion, orientation, and environmental condition monitoring** across factories, warehouses, logistics hubs, and smart industrial environments.

Equipped with an advanced **IMU for motion, tilt, shock, and orientation sensing**, along with **temperature, humidity, and barometric pressure sensors**, Ble Tag X2 captures both **asset movement and surrounding environmental conditions** in a single compact device. This enables accurate tracking of equipment usage, handling events, storage conditions, and operational anomalies — without any wired infrastructure.

Built for **low-power, long-term deployments**, Ble Tag X2 operates on a **battery-powered architecture** and continuously broadcasts sensor data using BLE, allowing seamless integration with gateways, smartphones, and industrial IoT platforms. It is ideal for **predictive maintenance, condition-based monitoring, and asset intelligence** in Industry 4.0 deployments.

With its **rugged industrial design and multi-sensor fusion capability**, Ble Tag X2 transforms physical asset behavior into actionable insights — helping organizations **improve operational efficiency, reduce asset losses, and maintain compliance** across critical industrial workflows.
