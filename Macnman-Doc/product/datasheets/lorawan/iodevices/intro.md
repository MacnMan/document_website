# MacSync-LX2-STD-PO

## Product Overview

**MacSync-LX2-STD-PO** is a high-performance **RS485 & Analog to LoRaWAN¬Æ** data logger, engineered for reliable industrial data acquisition in demanding environments. Designed for seamless integration with a wide range of MODBUS sensors, industrial meters, PLCs, transducers, and analog instruments, this device converts field data into long-range wireless LoRaWAN¬Æ packets with exceptional accuracy and stability.

Equipped with **switchable RS485 / 4‚Äì20 mA / 0‚Äì10 V channels**, the MacSync-LX2-STD-PO adapts to virtually any industrial monitoring application‚Äîeliminating the need for multiple product variants. Its rugged enclosure, industrial-grade components, and ultra-stable power architecture make it ideal for factories, utilities, agriculture, process industries, environmental monitoring, cold storage, building automation, and remote asset telemetry.

With support for LoRaWAN¬Æ v1.0.4, Macnman MacTalk Protocol, Class A & C modes, and an internal high-efficiency antenna, it offers ultra-long-range communication, minimal power consumption, and carrier-grade reliability. The device also includes secure onboarding, OTA configuration via BLE (Maya App), and optional onboard data buffering for fail-safe operation even during network outages.

Whether you‚Äôre building a smart factory, deploying large-scale IoT networks, or retrofitting legacy equipment, MacSync-LX2-STD-PO delivers seamless, scalable, and future-proof connectivity‚Äîturning any industrial sensor into a smart LoRaWAN¬Æ endpoint.
