# MacSync-WX1-PO

## Product Overview

**MacSync-WX1-PO** is an industrial-grade **RS485 & Analog to Wi-Fi data logger**, designed for reliable data acquisition and real-time communication in industrial and commercial environments where Wi-Fi connectivity is available. It retains the proven hardware architecture of the MacSync data logger family with **direct IP-based Wi-Fi connectivity**.

The device seamlessly interfaces with **MODBUS RTU sensors, energy meters, PLCs, transmitters, and industrial instruments**, acquiring data from **RS485, 4‚Äì20 mA, 0‚Äì10 V, and digital inputs**. Collected data is securely transmitted to **on‚Äëpremise servers, local dashboards, or cloud platforms** using standard protocols such as **MQTT, HTTP/HTTPS, and TCP/IP**, without requiring any external gateways.

Built for continuous 24√ó7 operation, MacSync-WX1-PO features a robust power-operated design, noise-immune circuitry, and an industrial enclosure suitable for factories, utilities, buildings, cold storage, and infrastructure monitoring. **Onboard data buffering and store‚Äëand‚Äëforward mechanisms** ensure data integrity even during temporary network or server outages.

Whether used for **industrial automation, energy monitoring, building management systems, process monitoring, or equipment health tracking**, **MacSync-WX1-PO delivers fast, reliable, and scalable Wi‚ÄëFi‚Äìbased data logging**, making it ideal for deployments that demand low latency, local control, and seamless IT/OT integration.
