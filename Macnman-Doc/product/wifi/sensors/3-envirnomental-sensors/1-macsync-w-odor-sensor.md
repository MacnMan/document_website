## Product Overview

**MacSync-W-Odor-X1** is a qualitative **Wi‚ÄëFi‚Äìbased odor monitoring sensor** designed to detect, track, and trend **odor intensity levels** in industrial, municipal, and commercial environments where reliable IP connectivity is available.
