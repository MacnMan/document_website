# MacSet-CX1

## Product Overview

MacSet-CX1 is a robust, power-operated **4G LTE Cellular Controller** designed for reliable remote control and automation of industrial and field equipment. Equipped with **2 high-current digital outputs**, it enables secure switching of pumps, motors, valves, alarms, solenoids, lighting systems, and other electrical loads over cellular connectivity — without requiring any local Wi-Fi, Ethernet, or LoRaWAN gateway infrastructure.

Engineered for demanding outdoor and industrial environments, the MacSet-CX1 features a stable **12–24 V DC** power architecture, noise-immune circuitry, and an **IP66-rated enclosure** suitable for harsh field conditions. Its always-on cellular link ensures dependable command delivery and monitoring across farms, industrial plants, buildings, and remote infrastructure sites.

The device supports cloud integration using **MQTT / HTTPS** and can be configured locally via **BLE using the Macnman Maya App**, enabling fast installation, testing, and diagnostics. It also supports local automation logic such as timers, schedules, and fail-safe output behavior for uninterrupted operations.

Whether used for smart agriculture, industrial automation, water management, or remote asset control, **MacSet-CX1 delivers a powerful, scalable, and future-ready solution for cellular-based switching and automation.**
