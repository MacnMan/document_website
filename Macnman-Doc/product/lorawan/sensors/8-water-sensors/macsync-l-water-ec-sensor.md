# MacSync LWQS X1 LoRaWAN Water Quality EC Sensor Datasheet

<h2 className="device-section-title"> 
  ## Product Overview
</h2>

**MacSync LWQS X1** is an industrial-grade **LoRaWAN® Water Quality Sensor** engineered for continuous online monitoring of **water electrical conductivity (EC)** across drinking water systems, wastewater treatment plants, industrial process water, cooling towers, reservoirs, rivers, lakes, borewells, and smart IoT water infrastructure.

Designed for high-accuracy and long-term deployment stability, the **LoRaWAN Water EC Sensor** continuously measures dissolved ionic concentration and water conductivity levels, helping industries and utilities monitor water quality, salinity variation, contamination risks, dissolved solids, and abnormal process conditions in real time.

Built around a precision industrial EC probe with advanced signal conditioning, **MacSync LWQS X1** delivers stable and repeatable conductivity measurements with minimal sensor drift even in harsh outdoor and submerged monitoring environments. The rugged industrial enclosure and IP-rated sensing architecture make the device ideal for industrial water quality monitoring applications requiring reliable long-term field performance.

Optimized for ultra-low-power operation and long-range wireless telemetry, the sensor securely transmits conductivity and water quality data over **LoRaWAN® networks**, enabling multi-year battery-powered operation for remote and unmanned deployments. The device supports real-time cloud monitoring, threshold-based alerts, predictive maintenance analytics, and centralized IoT-based water management systems.

With support for **LoRaWAN® v1.0.4**, AES-encrypted wireless communication, OTA configuration using the **Macnman Maya App**, and compatibility with ChirpStack, TTN, MQTT, LORIOT, Orbiwise, and private LoRaWAN® infrastructures, **MacSync LWQS X1** provides a scalable and future-ready solution for **wireless water quality monitoring and industrial water EC analytics**.
