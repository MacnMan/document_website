# MacSync LWQS X2 LoRaWAN Water pH Sensor Datasheet

<h2 className="device-section-title"> 
  ## Product Overview
</h2>

**MacSync LWQS X2** is an industrial-grade **LoRaWAN® Water pH Sensor** engineered for continuous online monitoring of water pH levels across drinking water systems, wastewater treatment plants (WWTP), sewage treatment plants (STP), industrial process water, cooling towers, reservoirs, rivers, lakes, borewells, aquaculture systems, and smart IoT water infrastructure.

Designed for high-accuracy and long-term deployment stability, the **LoRaWAN Water pH Sensor** continuously measures water acidity and alkalinity levels in real time, helping industries and utilities maintain water quality compliance, optimize chemical dosing, detect contamination risks, and monitor critical process conditions.

Built around a precision industrial electrochemical pH probe with advanced signal conditioning, **MacSync LWQS X2** delivers stable and repeatable pH measurements with minimal sensor drift even in harsh outdoor and submerged monitoring environments. The rugged industrial enclosure and IP-rated sensing architecture make the device ideal for industrial water quality monitoring applications requiring reliable long-term field performance.

Optimized for ultra-low-power operation and long-range wireless telemetry, the sensor securely transmits pH and water quality data over **LoRaWAN® networks**, enabling multi-year battery-powered operation for remote and unmanned deployments. The device supports real-time cloud monitoring, threshold-based alerts, predictive maintenance analytics, and centralized IoT-based water management systems.

With support for **LoRaWAN® v1.0.4**, AES-encrypted wireless communication, OTA configuration using the **Macnman Maya App**, and compatibility with ChirpStack, TTN, MQTT, Orbiwise, LORIOT, and private LoRaWAN® infrastructures, **MacSync LWQS X2** provides a scalable and future-ready solution for **wireless water quality monitoring and industrial water pH analytics**.
