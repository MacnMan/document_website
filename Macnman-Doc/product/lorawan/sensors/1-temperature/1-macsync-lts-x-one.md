# MacSync LTS X1 LoRaWAN® Temperature Humidity Sensor

<h2 className="device-section-title">
  ## Product Overview
</h2>

**MacSync-LTS X1** is an industrial-grade **LoRaWAN® temperature humidity sensor** designed for accurate environmental monitoring across smart industries, agriculture, cold chain logistics, warehouses, HVAC systems, greenhouses, pharmaceutical storage, and outdoor monitoring applications. Built for ultra-low-power operation and long-range wireless communication, the device continuously measures **temperature, humidity, and optional barometric pressure** and securely transmits real-time sensor data over LoRaWAN® networks.

Engineered for harsh industrial and outdoor environments, the MacSync-LTS X1 combines high-accuracy sensing elements, rugged IP-rated enclosure protection, and advanced thermal isolation architecture to deliver stable and reliable measurements even in extreme weather conditions. The sensor is ideal for applications requiring continuous **wireless temperature and humidity monitoring** with minimal maintenance and multi-year battery life.

As a professional **LoRaWAN temperature sensor** and **LoRaWAN humidity sensor**, the MacSync-LTS X1 supports ultra-long-range communication, enabling large-scale environmental monitoring deployments across factories, smart cities, farms, tunnels, telecom infrastructure, cold rooms, and remote industrial assets. Its optimized internal antenna and industrial LoRa® radio architecture ensure robust connectivity in challenging RF environments.

The device supports **LoRaWAN® v1.0.4**, Class A operation, OTAA activation, AES-encrypted communication, and optional BLE configuration through the Maya App for simplified deployment, diagnostics, and OTA parameter configuration. The MacSync-LTS X1 is compatible with major public and private LoRaWAN® network servers including ChirpStack, The Things Stack, LORIOT, MQTT platforms, and custom industrial IoT infrastructures.

Whether you are deploying a **smart agriculture temperature humidity sensor**, an industrial environmental monitoring system, or a low-power wireless climate monitoring network, the MacSync-LTS X1 delivers carrier-grade reliability, scalable connectivity, and real-time climate intelligence for next-generation IoT applications.
