# Narmada TSX1 LoRaWAN® Temperature & Humidity Sensor

<h2 className="device-section-title">
  ## Product Overview
</h2>

**Narmada TSX1** is a compact industrial-grade **LoRaWAN® temperature humidity sensor** engineered for smart buildings, factories, HVAC systems, offices, warehouses, hospitals, retail infrastructure, server rooms, and wireless indoor environmental monitoring applications. Designed for modern Industrial IoT deployments, the device continuously measures **temperature, humidity, and barometric pressure** while transmitting real-time environmental data over ultra-long-range LoRaWAN® wireless networks with extremely low power consumption.

Built using precision industrial sensing technology and an ultra-low-power wireless architecture, the **Narmada TSX1 LoRaWAN® temperature humidity sensor** delivers highly stable environmental monitoring with fast response times, low drift characteristics, and long-term operational reliability. Its compact enclosure, lightweight design, and flexible installation options make it ideal for HVAC automation, smart office infrastructure, factory climate monitoring, cold storage facilities, laboratories, clean rooms, production areas, smart warehouses, and Industry 4.0 environmental analytics.

The device supports **LoRaWAN® v1.0.4 Class A communication**, AES-128 encrypted wireless transmission, BLE-based configuration through the Macnman Maya App, and intelligent uplink strategies including periodic, threshold-based, and event-driven reporting. Optimized for multi-year battery-powered operation, the Narmada TSX1 enables scalable wireless temperature and humidity monitoring deployments without expensive wiring infrastructure.

Whether deployed for building automation, indoor climate monitoring, HVAC optimization, industrial environmental analytics, workplace comfort monitoring, predictive maintenance systems, or smart facility management, the **Narmada TSX1 industrial LoRaWAN® environmental sensor** transforms environmental conditions into actionable operational intelligence for connected buildings, factories, and next-generation smart infrastructure.

The **Narmada TSX1 wireless temperature humidity sensor** is fully compatible with major LoRaWAN® gateways and network servers including ChirpStack, The Things Stack, MQTT platforms, Orbiwise, and private industrial LoRaWAN® infrastructures — enabling seamless integration into Industrial IoT dashboards, SCADA systems, cloud monitoring platforms, and smart building ecosystems.
