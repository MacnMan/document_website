# MacSync LSS X4 LoRaWAN Soil pH Sensor Datasheet

<h2 className="device-section-title">
  ## Product Overview
</h2>

**MacSync LSS X4** is an industrial-grade **LoRaWAN® soil pH sensor** designed for precision agriculture, smart farming, greenhouse automation, plantations, vineyards, orchards, crop monitoring, and long-range environmental soil analysis applications. Built for ultra-low-power remote deployments, the sensor continuously measures **soil pH levels** in real time to help farmers and agricultural integrators optimize soil chemistry, fertilizer usage, nutrient absorption, and crop productivity.

The device enables continuous monitoring of soil acidity and alkalinity across agricultural fields, helping growers maintain balanced soil conditions for healthier crop development and improved irrigation and fertilization strategies.

Engineered using Macnman’s low-power wireless architecture, the **MacSync LSS X4 LoRaWAN soil pH monitoring sensor** provides stable and accurate soil pH measurements across multiple soil environments including clay soil, sandy soil, red soil, cocopeat, greenhouse media, plantation soil, and high-moisture agricultural fields.

Its rugged IP67/IP68-ready outdoor enclosure and corrosion-resistant industrial soil pH electrode enable long-term deployment in harsh agricultural and environmental conditions. The sensor supports seamless integration with **The Things Network (TTN), ChirpStack, MQTT servers, Loriot, Orbiwise, private LoRaWAN® gateways, and industrial IoT dashboards.**

The device supports configurable uplink intervals, threshold-based soil pH alerts, OTA configuration via the Maya App, local BLE diagnostics, onboard data buffering, and multi-year battery operation for remote farmland deployments.

MacSync LSS X4 is ideal for:

- Precision agriculture and smart farming
- Soil pH monitoring and soil chemistry analysis
- Fertilizer optimization and nutrient management
- Greenhouse and polyhouse farming
- Plantation and commercial agriculture
- Vineyard and orchard soil monitoring
- Soil health analytics and research
- Remote agricultural telemetry systems
- Smart irrigation and fertigation projects
- Agricultural universities and research farms
- Landscape and smart city soil monitoring
- Long-range LoRaWAN® environmental monitoring
