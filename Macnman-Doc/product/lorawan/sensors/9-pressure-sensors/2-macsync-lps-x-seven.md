# MacSync LPS X7 LoRaWAN® Hydro-Static Liquid Level Sensor

<h2 className="device-section-title">
  ## Product Overview 
</h2>

**MacSync LPS X7** is an industrial-grade **LoRaWAN® hydro-static liquid level sensor** engineered for accurate low-pressure liquid-level monitoring across soap dispensers, water bottles, diesel containers, chemical tanks, small reservoirs, liquid storage systems, and smart IoT monitoring infrastructure.

Designed specifically for small liquid-column pressure sensing applications, the device continuously measures hydro-static pressure variations generated by liquid levels and wirelessly transmits real-time telemetry over ultra-long-range LoRaWAN® networks.

Built using a precision hydro-static sensing architecture with industrial-grade stainless-steel construction, the MacSync LPS X7 delivers stable and reliable liquid-level analytics even in moisture-rich, corrosive, vibration-prone, and outdoor industrial environments.

Its rugged IP65/IP67 industrial enclosure, ultra-low-power electronics, and long-range LoRaWAN® wireless communication enable large-scale distributed liquid-level monitoring deployments without complex wired infrastructure.

The sensor is ideal for smart soap dispenser monitoring, water bottle level monitoring, small diesel tank monitoring, beverage container monitoring, industrial liquid reservoir monitoring, restroom utility analytics, remote utility telemetry, and smart facility management deployments.

MacSync LPS X7 supports BLE-based configuration through the Macnman Maya App, secure OTA configuration, event-based liquid-level reporting, threshold alerts, and seamless integration with LoRaWAN® gateways, MQTT platforms, SCADA systems, industrial IoT dashboards, and smart building management systems.

Whether deployed in smart buildings, industrial facilities, public infrastructure, utility monitoring systems, hospitality environments, fuel storage systems, or remote liquid monitoring installations, the **MacSync LPS X7 LoRaWAN® liquid-level monitoring sensor** transforms real-time liquid telemetry into actionable operational intelligence.
