# MacSync LOXGS X1 LoRaWAN Oxygen Sensor Datasheet

<h2 className="device-section-title">
  ## Product Overview
</h2>


**MacSync LOXGS X1 LoRaWAN® Oxygen Sensor** is a high-performance industrial wireless oxygen sensor designed for continuous real-time oxygen concentration monitoring across factories, confined spaces, hospitals, laboratories, industrial plants, smart buildings, and hazardous environments.

Built using ultra-low-power electronics and long-range **LoRaWAN® wireless technology**, the sensor enables reliable remote oxygen monitoring without requiring complex cabling infrastructure. The device continuously measures ambient oxygen levels and helps organizations detect oxygen depletion, oxygen enrichment, ventilation failures, gas displacement risks, and unsafe atmospheric conditions before they become critical.

The **LoRaWAN Oxygen Sensor** is engineered for Industrial IoT deployments where long battery life, wide-area coverage, secure wireless communication, and centralized cloud monitoring are essential. It is ideal for industrial safety monitoring, oxygen deficiency detection, confined space monitoring, gas storage facilities, manufacturing plants, medical oxygen monitoring systems, research laboratories, and environmental monitoring applications.

With support for configurable uplink intervals, threshold-based alerts, remote diagnostics, BLE configuration via the **Macnman Maya App**, and seamless integration with major LoRaWAN® gateways and IoT cloud platforms, the **MacSync LOXGS X1** delivers scalable oxygen analytics for Industry 4.0 infrastructure.

Its rugged industrial enclosure, IP65 protection, low-power battery operation, and secure AES-encrypted communication make it suitable for harsh indoor and outdoor industrial deployments where reliable wireless oxygen monitoring is critical.
