# MacSync-LH2S-X1 Datasheet

## Product Overview

**MacSync-LH2S-X1** is an industrial-grade **LoRaWAN® Hydrogen Sulfide (H₂S) gas sensor** designed for **continuous, quantitative monitoring of H₂S concentration in ppm** across industrial, municipal, and environmental applications.

Built around a **high-accuracy electrochemical H₂S sensing element**, the device delivers reliable, stable, and repeatable gas concentration measurements, enabling early detection of toxic gas buildup and proactive safety intervention. Hydrogen sulfide is a highly hazardous and corrosive gas commonly present in **sewage systems, wastewater treatment plants, industrial processes, landfills, and confined spaces**, making accurate monitoring critical for worker safety and regulatory compliance.

MacSync-LH2S-X1 operates on an **ultra‑low‑power LoRaWAN® Class A architecture**, providing **long-range wireless connectivity and multi‑year battery life** even in remote or unmanned locations. Its rugged, ingress‑protected enclosure ensures dependable operation in harsh outdoor and industrial environments.

The sensor supports **threshold-based alerts, trend analysis, and periodic reporting**, allowing seamless integration with **SCADA systems, safety dashboards, and cloud platforms** over public or private LoRaWAN® networks. Optional **BLE‑based configuration via the Macnman Maya App** simplifies installation, diagnostics, and maintenance.

From **STP / WWTP safety monitoring and sewer gas detection** to **industrial process safety and confined-space monitoring**, **MacSync-LH2S-X1 transforms toxic H₂S gas data into actionable, data‑driven insights**.
