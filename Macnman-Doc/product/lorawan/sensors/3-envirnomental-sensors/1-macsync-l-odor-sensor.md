# MacSync-L-Odor-X1 Datasheet

## Product Overview

**MacSync-L-Odor-X1** is a qualitative **LoRaWAN¬Æ odor monitoring sensor** designed to detect, track, and trend **odor intensity levels** in industrial, municipal, and outdoor environments. Instead of measuring individual gas concentrations in ppm, the device evaluates overall odor presence and strength, making it ideal for applications where **nuisance odor detection, early warning, and compliance monitoring** are critical.

Built on advanced sensing elements and intelligent signal processing, MacSync-L-Odor-X1 converts complex odor signatures into a **simple odor index / odor level**, enabling easy interpretation and actionable insights. This qualitative approach allows operators to identify odor spikes, persistent odor patterns, and abnormal events without requiring gas-specific calibration or complex analysis.

Engineered for long-term field deployment, the device features an **ultra‚Äëlow‚Äëpower LoRaWAN¬Æ Class A architecture**, ensuring multi‚Äëyear battery life even in remote or unmanned locations. Its rugged, ingress‚Äëprotected enclosure makes it suitable for harsh environments such as **sewage treatment plants (STP), wastewater facilities (WWTP), landfills, waste processing units, factories, and smart city infrastructure**.

With support for **LoRaWAN¬Æ v1.0.4**, AES‚Äëencrypted communication, wide regional frequency bands, and optional **BLE‚Äëbased configuration via the Maya App**, MacSync-L-Odor-X1 enables fast deployment, remote monitoring, and scalable fleet management across public or private LoRaWAN¬Æ networks.
