# MacSet LX2 Industrial® LoRaWAN Controller

<h2 className="device-section-title">
  ## Product Overview
</h2>

**MacSet LX2** is an industrial-grade **LoRaWAN® Multi-IO Controller** engineered for advanced remote automation, industrial equipment control, multi-channel digital output switching, analog and digital input monitoring, and wireless Industrial IoT deployments. Designed for large-scale industrial automation and infrastructure control, the controller enables reliable long-range wireless operation of pumps, motors, solenoid valves, alarms, contactors, lighting systems, actuators, industrial panels, and field equipment over secure LoRaWAN® networks.

Built for demanding industrial and outdoor operating environments, the **MacSet LX2 LoRaWAN Controller** combines **8 high-current digital outputs**, **8 configurable AI/DI interfaces**, **RS485 Modbus RTU communication**, and intelligent automation logic inside a rugged industrial IP-rated enclosure. The controller is ideal for industrial process automation, smart agriculture systems, utility automation, water infrastructure, factory automation, SCADA integration, remote pumping stations, energy management systems, environmental monitoring, and unmanned industrial installations.

With support for **LoRaWAN® v1.0.4**, Class A and Class C operation, the controller delivers ultra-long-range wireless communication with optimized power efficiency for highly scalable Industrial IoT deployments. The integrated **Macnman MacTalk Protocol** enables direct peer-to-peer device communication and local automation workflows without requiring cloud connectivity, reducing latency and improving reliability for mission-critical industrial automation systems.

The controller supports flexible industrial interfacing including **8 high-current digital outputs**, **1 × RS485 Modbus RTU interface**, and **8 configurable AI/DI channels** supporting analog and digital input monitoring. This enables seamless integration with industrial sensors, PLC systems, SCADA infrastructure, process instruments, VFDs, industrial meters, automation panels, and industrial control systems.

Optimized for Industrial IoT scalability, the device supports secure AES-encrypted communication, OTA configuration using the **Macnman Maya App**, remote parameter management, event-driven automation logic, threshold-based actuation, cyclic automation workflows, and compatibility with major LoRaWAN® platforms including ChirpStack, TTN, MQTT, Orbiwise, LORIOT, and private industrial LoRaWAN® servers.

Whether deployed for **industrial automation**, **remote pump control**, **smart irrigation systems**, **industrial process monitoring**, **factory automation**, **utility infrastructure control**, **wireless digital output switching**, or **edge-based Industrial IoT automation systems**, **MacSet LX2** delivers a scalable, reliable, and future-ready solution for modern industrial wireless automation infrastructure.

<h2 className="device-section-title">
  ## What's in the Box?
</h2>

The **MacSet LX2 Industrial LoRaWAN® IO Controller** package is designed to simplify industrial deployment, remote automation installation, and Industrial IoT commissioning. Before starting installation, verify that all supplied accessories and mounting components are available inside the package.

The standard package contents include:

- MacSet LX2 Industrial LoRaWAN® IO Controller
- Industrial Mounting Clip
- Quick Installation & User Manual
- Product Warranty Card
- Mounting Screws & Fasteners
- IP68 Cable Extender \*
- External Power Adapter \*

The included mounting accessories help simplify installation inside industrial automation panels, factory control systems, utility infrastructure, water management systems, smart agriculture installations, pump control panels, SCADA systems, and outdoor Industrial IoT deployments.

Ensure all accessories and installation components are inspected before powering the device or starting field deployment.

![MacSet LX2 industrial LoRaWAN IO controller package contents and installation accessories](/img/wifi/controllers/macset-wx-wifi-controller-whats-in-the-box.svg)

:::note

Optional accessories including IP68 cable extenders, external power adapters, and additional industrial mounting accessories may not be included in the standard package and can be purchased separately based on deployment requirements.

:::

<h2 className="device-section-title">
  ## MacSet LX1 Controller Dimensions
</h2>

The **MacSet LX2 Industrial LoRaWAN® IO Controller** is designed with a rugged industrial enclosure optimized for Industrial IoT automation, remote equipment control, wireless monitoring, and distributed industrial infrastructure deployments. With compact dimensions of **142 mm × 80 mm × 32 mm**, the controller enables easy installation inside industrial automation panels, electrical cabinets, utility control rooms, pump control systems, smart agriculture infrastructure, factory automation systems, and outdoor field deployments.
