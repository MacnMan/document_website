## Conditions and Assumptions

### Compliance
- Design will comply with applicable **CE** requirements for industrial electronics.  
- Wi‑Fi RF operation will comply with applicable **regional wireless regulations** (pre‑compliance scope).  
- Final certification and authority approvals, if required, are outside the current project scope unless explicitly stated.

### Unique Serial Code
- Each deployed edge controller and sensing unit will carry a **unique serial number** for identification.  
- Serial numbers will be used for installation records, calibration mapping, maintenance tracking, and data correlation.  

### Site & Infrastructure Assumptions
- Adequate **physical mounting locations** are available for LiDAR sensors, cameras, and edge controllers near the track.  
- Stable power availability is provided at the installation location.  
- Line‑of‑sight or acceptable mounting geometry is feasible for accurate wagon profiling and speed detection.  
- Necessary permissions for trackside installation will be arranged by the customer.

### Manufacturing & Procurement
- Availability of LiDAR sensors, AI cameras, and industrial networking components is subject to supplier lead times.  
- Any delays caused by global supply constraints may impact the project schedule.  
- Component substitutions due to EOL or non‑availability will be proposed with equivalent or better specifications and will require **customer approval**.

### Data & Accuracy Assumptions
- Wagon load estimation is based on **relative 3D profiling** and is not intended to replace certified weighbridge systems.  
- Accuracy is dependent on correct installation, calibration, and environmental conditions.  
- Extreme dust accumulation or physical obstructions may temporarily affect measurement accuracy.

### Scope Changes
- Any request beyond the defined scope—such as additional analytics, third‑party system integrations, dashboard customization, or production‑scale rollouts—will be treated as a **separate scope of work**.  
- Scope changes may impact commercial terms, timelines, and delivery milestones and will require formal written approval.
