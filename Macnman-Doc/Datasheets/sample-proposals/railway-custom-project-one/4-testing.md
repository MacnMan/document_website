## Bill of Materials (BoM) Optimization
- Prototype‑stage BoM preparation for trackside sensing hardware<br/>
- Cost, availability, and environmental suitability evaluation<br/>
- Post‑field test BoM optimization for scalability and cost reduction

## Certification and Compliance
### CE Certification
- EMC, EMI, and electrical safety compliance (CE pre‑certification)

### WPC Certification
- Wi‑Fi RF compliance as per applicable regional regulations

### IP67 Rating (Not in Scope)
- Dust and water ingress protection (IP67) — Not included in current scope
