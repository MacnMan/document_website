# LoRaWAN Hub – Technical Specifications

## Entrance & Exit AI LPR Pro Bullet Plus Camera – Technical Specifications

**Application Role:**  
- Train / locomotive number identification at entry and exit points  
- Association of train ID with wagon count and load analytics  

**Imaging & AI Capabilities:**  
- AI-powered License / Number Plate Recognition (LPR)  
- Supports **Black List** and **White List** based identification rules  
- **No-plate vehicle detection** for incomplete or obscured number plates  
- High accuracy recognition under varying train speeds  

**Optical & Image Performance:**  
- **140dB Super WDR** for extreme lighting conditions (backlight, glare, night operation)  
- Optimized for outdoor, high-contrast railway environments  
- IR-assisted imaging for low-light and night-time operation  

**Triggering & Integration:**  
- Event-based image capture synchronized with LiDAR-based train detection  
- Metadata tagging with timestamp and event ID  
- Seamless integration with MacSync W Series edge controller  

**Environmental & Mechanical Protection:**  
- **IP67** rated enclosure for dust and water ingress protection  
- **IK10** vandal-resistant housing suitable for harsh outdoor installations  
- Designed for continuous outdoor operation in rail-side environments  

**Communication & Output:**  
- Ethernet-based video and metadata output  
- Supports ONVIF / RTSP streams (where applicable)  
- Event data forwarded to edge controller for correlation and storage  

**Mounting & Deployment:**  
- Bullet-style form factor for fixed entrance / exit mounting  
- Adjustable mounting bracket for precise alignment  
- Suitable for pole, wall, or gantry mounting  

**Reliability & Maintenance:**  
- Continuous operation with health monitoring  
- Supports remote diagnostics and firmware upgrades (camera-side)
